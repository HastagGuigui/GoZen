About

What is GoZen?

GoZen is a modular video editor made with Godot. The goal which GoZen is trying to achieve is to become the best open source video editor.

Modular video editor?

With modular we mean that everything about this editor can be replaced by custom modules, from the timeline, to even the window features such as the top bar. Because the editor is made in Godot, it is easy for people without too much programming experience to create their own modules.

In short, you can add more functionality, change the style, change default modules, and really make this video editor fit your personal feel and style.
